# Gift Story — Product Pricing Template

## 1. Purpose

This file is a practical pricing template for every Gift Story box or gift story before it is uploaded to Salla, Zid, delivery apps, or any sales channel.

No product should go live before this template is completed.

The goal is to make sure every price protects:

* Actual cost.
* Packaging cost.
* Payment fees.
* Platform fees.
* Delivery app commissions if applicable.
* Target profit.
* Brand perception.
* Channel-specific pricing.

## 5. Landed Cost Rule

Product cost should be recorded as landed cost.

Landed cost means the real cost of the product after including sourcing, travel, shipping, customs, or any related cost if already included.

Do not count the same shipping or customs cost twice.

Correct:

```text
Product landed cost = full real product cost
```

Wrong:

```text
Product price + shipping/customs again when already included
```

## 15. 299 SAR Check

299 SAR is allowed only when the cost supports the target profit.

It is not the fixed price for all boxes.

Before using 299 SAR, check:

```text
Total cost supports 50% profit above cost
Packaging cost is included
Payment fee is included
Product value looks appropriate
Photos support the price
Channel fees do not weaken margin
```

Using the current direct payment assumption:

```text
Payment fee = 7% + 1 SAR
Price = 299 SAR
```

Estimated net after payment:

```text
299 × 0.93 - 1 = 277.07 SAR
```

Maximum total cost to protect the 50% profit-above-cost target:

```text
277.07 ÷ 1.5 = 184.71 SAR
```

Rule:

```text
A direct-platform box priced at 299 SAR should usually have total cost around 184 SAR or below under the 7% + 1 SAR fee assumption.
```

## 25. Example Pricing Case

Example only:

```text
Box ID: GS-LS-D01-001

Packaging Cost: 18 SAR
Preparation Cost: 0 SAR
Delivery / Shipping Cost: 0 SAR

Minimum Net Revenue Needed: 252 SAR

Suggested Direct Price: 299 SAR
Final Salla / Zid Price: 299 SAR
Expected Net After Payment: 299 × 0.93 - 1 = 277.07 SAR
Expected Profit: 277.07 - 168 = 109.07 SAR
Profit Above Cost %: 64.9%
Status: Approved
```

Decision:

```text
299 SAR is acceptable in this example because it protects more than 50% profit above total cost.
```
