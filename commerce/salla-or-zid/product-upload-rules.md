# Gift Story — Product Upload Rules for Salla / Zid

## 1. Purpose

This file defines how Gift Story products should be uploaded to Salla, Zid, or any approved commerce platform.

The goal is to keep product listings:

* Premium.
* Clear.
* Consistent.
* Correctly priced.
* Easy to understand.
* Ready for customer purchase.
* Aligned with Gift Story’s brand identity.

No product should be uploaded before it is costed, photographed, approved, and ready to sell.

---

## 2. Core Rule

The commerce platform is the official place for:

* Active products.
* Prices.
* Inventory.
* Checkout.
* Payment.
* Orders.
* Customer purchase details.

The official website introduces the brand.
The commerce platform completes the sale.

---

## 3. Product Must Be Approved Before Upload

Before uploading any product, confirm:

```text
Product is physically available
Product has landed cost recorded
Box cost is calculated
Packaging cost is included
Payment/platform fees are considered
Final price is approved
Product photos are clean
Description is ready
Inventory quantity is correct
Delivery/preparation note is clear
Policy note is included
```

Do not upload draft products publicly.

---

## 4. Active Launch Category

The first active category is:

**هدايا الصغار**
**Little Stories — Baby & Kids Gifts**

This should be the main category inside the commerce platform during launch.

Other categories may be added later, but should not distract from the first launch.

---

## 5. Product Naming Rule

Product names should feel elegant, clear, and aligned with Gift Story.

Recommended format:

```text
[Category Name] — [Story Name / Edition]
```

Examples:

```text
هدايا الصغار — القصة الناعمة
هدايا الصغار — إصدار المولود
Little Story Box — Soft Baby Edition
Little Stories — Neutral Baby Gift
```

Avoid:

```text
بوكس أطفال
بوكس مواليد رقم 1
عرض أطفال
سلة أطفال
منتجات أطفال متنوعة
هدية رخيصة
بوكس بسعر خاص
```

The name should feel like a gift story, not a random product bundle.

---

## 6. Product Title Length

Product title should be:

* Clear.
* Short.
* Searchable.
* Elegant.

Recommended maximum:

```text
4 to 8 words
```

Good example:

```text
هدايا الصغار — إصدار ناعم
```

Bad example:

```text
بوكس أطفال فاخر فيه ملابس وأشياء كثيرة هدية مولود بنات وأولاد
```

## 7. Product Category Setup

Recommended starting categories in the commerce platform:

```text

```

Optional later categories:

```text
قصص العناية
```

Coming Soon categories should stay on the official website until products are ready to sell.

## 8. Product Description Structure

Each product description should follow this structure:

```text
1. Short emotional opening
2. What the gift is suitable for
3. What the experience includes
4. Availability note
5. Packaging note
6. Gift card note
7. Policy note
```

The description should be clear and premium, not too long.

---

Use this for quick product summary.

```text
قصة هدية ناعمة من Gift Story، منسقة بعناية للمولود أو الطفل، وتصل بتغليف أنيق وكرت إهداء لتكون جاهزة للتقديم.
```

Alternative:

```text
هدية منسقة بعناية من هدايا الصغار، بتفاصيل ناعمة وتغليف Gift Story الجاهز للتقديم.
```

---

## 10. Full Product Description Template

Use this structure for product pages.

```text
هذه القصة من هدايا الصغار صُممت لتكون هدية ناعمة، مرتبة، وجاهزة للتقديم.

تناسب تهنئة المولود، زيارة أم جديدة، أو مناسبة عائلية صغيرة تحتاج هدية أنيقة بدون إعادة تغليف أو تنسيق.

تصل الهدية داخل تجربة Gift Story، مع تغليف أنيق، ترتيب داخلي ناعم، وكرت إهداء حسب الرسالة المعتمدة من العميل.

قد تختلف بعض تفاصيل المحتوى حسب توفر القطع، مع الحفاظ على نفس مستوى التنسيق والفخامة في كل قصة.
```

---

## 11. What to Mention in Description

Allowed to mention:

* Category.
* Occasion.
* General style.
* Age/size if relevant.
* Color tone if confirmed.
* Gift experience.
* Packaging.
* Gift card.
* Limited availability.
* Preparation time.
* Product variation note.

Do not mention:

* Purchase source.
* Outlet.
* Discount.
* Ross.
* MSRP.
* Dollar price.
* Internal cost.
* Supplier details.
* Any private sourcing information.

---

## 12. Product Image Rules

Commerce product images must be customer-facing.

Allowed images:

```text
Clean product display image
Gift box image
Packaging image
Flat lay image
Close-up detail image
Gift card image without private message
```

Images must not show:

```text
Price tags
Store labels
Discount stickers
Ross
MSRP
Dollar signs
Purchase source
Supplier screenshots
Messy background
Personal information
Raw inventory photos
```

---

## 13. Product Image Order

Recommended image order:

```text
1. Main gift presentation image
2. Box or packaging image
3. Product arrangement image
4. Detail image
5. Gift card / packaging detail
6. Optional size or contents reference
```

The first image should make the product feel gift-ready.

Do not use a raw product-only image as the main image unless it has been edited to match Gift Story’s visual style.

---

## 14. Image Background Rule

Preferred backgrounds:

```text
Off-white
Creamy beige
Soft nude
White
Neutral fabric or surface
```

Avoid:

```text
Busy backgrounds
Dark harsh backgrounds
Store shelves
Retail tags
Floor photos
Unedited home photos
Supplier screenshots
Strong colors
```

---

## 15. Pricing Rule Before Upload

No product should be uploaded before final price approval.

Price must consider:

```text
Landed product cost
Packaging cost
Preparation cost
Payment fee
Platform fee
Delivery app fee if applicable
Target profit
Final channel price
```

Minimum profit target:

```text
50% above total cost
```

Formula:

```text
Minimum Net Revenue Needed = Total Cost × 1.5
```

---

## 16. Payment Fee Assumption

Working assumption for direct platform sales:

```text
Payment fee = 7% + 1 SAR
```

Formula:

```text
Minimum Direct Selling Price = (Total Cost × 1.5 + 1) ÷ 0.93
```

This assumption must be updated when actual platform/payment gateway terms are confirmed.

## 18. 299 SAR Rule

299 SAR is allowed as a price tier only when the cost supports it.

It is not the fixed price for all boxes.

Before using 299 SAR, confirm:

```text
Total cost supports target profit
Packaging cost is included
Payment fee is included
Product value looks appropriate
Photos support the price
Customer perception is premium
```

---

## 19. Inventory Quantity Rule

Each product listing must have accurate inventory.

Do not list products that are not physically available.

Recommended inventory setup:

```text
Quantity available = actual ready quantity
```

If every box is unique, use low quantities and update quickly after sale.

For one-of-one boxes:

```text
Quantity = 1
```

Once sold:

```text
Mark as Sold Out
```

Do not oversell unique boxes.

---

## 20. Product Status Rules

Use these internal statuses before public upload:

```text
Draft
Costed
Ready for Photo
Ready to Sell
Live
Sold Out
Paused
Archived
```

Only products marked as:

```text
Ready to Sell
```

can be uploaded publicly.

---

## 21. SKU / Product Code Rule

Each product should have a simple internal code.

Recommended format:

```text
GS-[CATEGORY]-[DROP]-[NUMBER]
```

Examples:

```text
GS-LS-D01-001
GS-LS-D01-002
GS-LS-D02-001
```

Meaning:

```text
GS = Gift Story
LS = Little Stories
D01 = Drop 01
001 = Product number
```

Keep SKU internal and consistent.

---

## 22. Product Options and Variants

Use variants only when truly needed.

Possible variants:

```text
Gift card message
Size if product is available in multiple sizes
Color if product is available in multiple colors
Delivery/preparation option
```

Avoid creating many variants if every box is unique.

For unique boxes, create separate listings instead of confusing variants.

---

## 23. Gift Card Message Field

If the platform supports custom fields, add:

```text
رسالة كرت الإهداء
```

Field note:

```text
اكتبي رسالة الإهداء هنا، وسيتم تجهيزها حسب النص المعتمد.
```

If message can be added later, write:

```text
يمكن إضافة رسالة كرت الإهداء الآن أو تأكيدها لاحقًا قبل التجهيز.
```

---

## 24. Preparation Time Note

Every product should include preparation time.

Suggested wording:

```text
يتم تجهيز الطلب بعناية بعد تأكيد الدفع وبيانات الطلب.
```

For ready products:

```text
القصص الجاهزة تحتاج وقت تجهيز حسب توفر الطلبات وطريقة التوصيل.
```

For special products:

```text
الطلبات الخاصة تحتاج وقتًا إضافيًا للمراجعة والتجهيز.
```

Do not promise same-day preparation unless operationally confirmed.

---

## 25. Limited Availability Note

Use this note when relevant:

```text
تتوفر هذه القصة بكميات محدودة، وقد لا تتكرر بنفس التفاصيل بعد نفاد القطع.
```

Avoid aggressive urgency like:

```text
الحق قبل لا تروح
آخر فرصة
اشترِ الآن فورًا
```

Gift Story scarcity should feel elegant, not pushy.

---

## 26. Product Variation Note

Use when contents may vary:

```text
قد تختلف بعض تفاصيل المحتوى حسب توفر القطع، مع الحفاظ على نفس مستوى التنسيق والفخامة في كل قصة.
```

This note should be visible but not alarming.

---

## 27. Packaging Note

Use this note:

```text
تصل الهدية بتغليف Gift Story، مع ترتيب داخلي أنيق وكرت إهداء، وحماية خارجية عند الحاجة للتوصيل أو الشحن.
```

If platform space is limited:

```text
تصل جاهزة للتقديم بتغليف Gift Story وكرت إهداء.
```

---

## 28. Return and Exchange Note

Use this policy note:

```text
نظرًا لطبيعة الهدايا المجهزة ومنتجات الأطفال والتغليف المخصص، لا يمكن استرجاع أو استبدال الطلب بعد خروجه من مقر التجهيز، حفاظًا على جودة المنتجات وسلامة تجربة الإهداء.
```

If the platform requires shorter text:

```text
لا يمكن الاسترجاع أو الاستبدال بعد خروج الطلب من مقر التجهيز، إلا في حال وجود خطأ واضح من Gift Story.
```

---

## 29. Color and Print Variation Note

Use this note when packaging visuals are shown:

```text
قد تختلف بعض التفاصيل اللونية بين العروض الرقمية والتغليف المطبوع، حيث تعتمد Gift Story في التغليف الفعلي على طباعة فاخرة بلون ذهبي موحد حسب مواصفات الإنتاج.
```

---


Important:

Shipping labels should not be placed on branded Gift Story packaging.

Before publishing, check:

```text

Category is correct
SKU is added
Price is approved
Inventory quantity is correct
Photos are clean
No tags/prices/source labels appear in images
Short description is ready
Full description is ready
Gift card field is added if available
Preparation note is included
Packaging note is included
Variation note is included if needed
Return/exchange note is included
Delivery settings are correct
Payment works
Product is tested on mobile
```

---

## 34. Product Page Review

After publishing, review the product page as a customer.

Check:

```text
Does the product feel premium?
Is the price clear?
Is the photo strong?
Is the description readable?
Is the CTA clear?
Is the gift experience understood?
Is the policy visible?
Is the page mobile-friendly?
Is there any confusing wording?
```

If the product page feels like a normal low-value listing, revise before launch.

---

## 35. Product Removal / Sold Out Rule

If the product sells out:

Recommended action:

```text
Mark as Sold Out
```

If it will not return:

```text
Move to archive or previous stories reference
```

Do not keep unavailable products looking purchasable.

If using sold-out products for brand story, move them to:

```text
Previous Stories
```

on the official website, not as active commerce products.

---

## 36. Product Duplication Rule

Do not duplicate the same unique box unless the exact same contents are available.

If a new box is inspired by a previous one, create a new listing.

Example:

```text
هدايا الصغار — إصدار ناعم 02
```

Do not claim it is the same story if details changed.

---

## 37. Channel Difference Rule

Salla/Zid prices may differ from delivery app prices.

Reason:

* Platform fees.
* Payment fees.
* Delivery app commission.
* Operational cost.
* Customer expectations.

Do not show conflicting prices on the official website.

The commerce platform should show its own approved price.

---

## 38. What Not to Upload

Do not upload:

```text
Products without cost
Products without approved price
Raw inventory photos
Products with visible retail tags
Products with visible source store
Products not physically available
Products that do not fit Gift Story style
Products with unclear condition
Products that do not fit packaging
Products with weak profit
Draft custom requests
Coming Soon products as purchasable
```

---

## 39. Final Rule

Every product uploaded to the commerce platform must protect:

1. Gift Story’s premium image.
2. Customer trust.
3. Pricing discipline.
4. Inventory accuracy.
5. Operational clarity.

A product is ready to upload only when it is:

```text
Costed
Photographed
Priced
Described
Available
Operationally ready
Aligned with Gift Story
```
