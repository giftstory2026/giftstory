# Gift Story — Homepage Copy

## Page Purpose

The homepage should introduce Gift Story as a premium gifting brand, explain the current active story, show the packaging experience, and guide customers to the active shop.

The homepage should not show detailed product prices.

---

## Internal Purpose

Create a premium first impression and explain Gift Story in one clear moment.

## Arabic Title

## Arabic Subtitle

## English Support Line

WHERE EVERY GIFT TELLS A STORY

## Primary CTA

تسوّقي القصة الحالية

## Secondary CTA

استكشفي عالم Gift Story

## Visual Direction

Use a soft premium image showing:

* Gift Story packaging.
* Outer gift bag.
* Inner box.
* Tissue paper.
* Gift card.
* Soft off-white / creamy background.

Avoid showing prices or raw product photos in the hero.

Do not show these sizes prominently unless needed in the packaging page.

# 12. Homepage Rules

## Do

* Keep the homepage soft and spacious.
* Use Arabic-first copy.
* Keep the shopping CTA clear.
* Highlight Little Stories as the active category.
* Show packaging as part of the value.
* Use Coming Soon categories lightly.
* Direct sales to the commerce platform.

## Avoid

* Showing detailed prices.
* Using discount language.
* Making WhatsApp the main ordering path.
* Showing raw product photos.
* Showing tags, prices, source stores, or MSRP.
* Overloading the page with too much text.
* Making the homepage look like a crowded store.
