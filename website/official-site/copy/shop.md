# Gift Story — Shop Redirect Page Copy

## Page Purpose

This page redirects customers from the official Gift Story website to the active commerce platform.

The page should feel official, calm, and premium.

It should not act as a product catalog.

It should not show detailed prices.

It should not replace the commerce platform checkout.

## Visual Direction

Use a simple premium visual showing:

* Gift Story packaging.
* Soft neutral background.
* Gift card or box detail.
* Calm transition feeling.

Avoid:

* Product grid.
* Price cards.
* Discount banners.
* WhatsApp-first messaging.
* Loud checkout graphics.

# 3. Why We Redirect

## Section Title

لماذا يتم الطلب عبر منصة البيع؟

## Body Copy

نستخدم منصة بيع رسمية لتنظيم الطلبات، عرض التوفر، تأكيد الأسعار، إدارة الدفع، ومتابعة تفاصيل الطلب بطريقة أوضح وأكثر احترافية.

الموقع الرسمي يعرّفكِ على عالم Gift Story، أما منصة البيع فهي المكان المخصص لإتمام الطلب.

## Points

* عرض القصص المتاحة حاليًا.
* توضيح الأسعار والتوفر.
* إتمام الدفع الرسمي.
* تسجيل بيانات الطلب.
* متابعة الطلب بطريقة منظمة.

## Internal Rule

Do not make WhatsApp the main CTA on this page.

The customer should be directed to:

1. The official commerce platform for ready products.
2. The Special Gift Story Request page for custom requests.
3. Contact page for general inquiries.

WhatsApp may be used only when needed for support or clarification.

# 10. Redirect Behavior

## Recommended Behavior

The page can work in one of two ways:

### Option 1 — Manual Button

The customer reads the transition message and clicks the button.

Recommended for launch because it feels clear and intentional.

### Option 2 — Auto Redirect

The page shows a short transition message, then redirects automatically after a few seconds.

If using auto redirect, keep a manual button available in case redirect fails.

## Suggested Auto Redirect Message

سيتم نقلكِ خلال لحظات إلى منصة البيع الرسمية الخاصة بـ Gift Story.

# 14. Page Rules

## Do

* Keep the page short and clear.
* Explain why the customer is being redirected.
* Use one strong shop CTA.
* Link to the official commerce platform.
* Provide a fallback if the shop is not ready.
* Keep the tone premium and calm.

## Avoid

* Showing product catalog here.
* Showing detailed prices.
* Creating duplicate checkout.
* Using WhatsApp as the main ordering path.
* Making the customer feel lost.
* Using discount language.
* Adding too many options.
