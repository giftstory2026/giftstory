# Gift Story — Homepage Wireframe

## 1. Purpose

This file defines the visual and structural wireframe for the Gift Story homepage.

The homepage should introduce the brand, explain the current story, show packaging value, and guide customers to the active commerce platform.

The homepage should feel premium, soft, clear, and spacious.

It should not feel like a crowded product catalog.

---

## 2. Homepage Goal

The homepage should answer these questions quickly:

1. What is Gift Story?
2. What does Gift Story sell?
3. What is the current active story?
4. Why is the packaging important?
5. How can the customer shop?
6. How can the customer request a special story?

---

## 3. Page Structure Overview

Recommended homepage order:

```text id="cazn3h"

5. What Makes Gift Story Different

```

# 16. Mobile Priority

The homepage should be mobile-first.

Mobile priorities:

* Hero image loads fast.
* CTA appears early.
* Text is short.
* Sections are not too tall.
* Cards stack cleanly.
* Form links are easy to tap.
* No crowded navigation.
* No heavy animations.
* Images are compressed.

# 19. Homepage Design Rules

## Do

* Keep the layout spacious.
* Use soft neutral backgrounds.
* Use muted gold accents.
* Make CTA clear.
* Show Little Stories as the active category.
* Make packaging feel valuable.
* Keep Coming Soon categories secondary.
* Use polished images only.
* Build trust before selling.

## Avoid

* Crowded product grids.
* Prices on homepage.
* WhatsApp-first CTA.
* Discount banners.
* Heavy animations.
* Strong navy identity.
* Raw inventory images.
* Too many icons.
* Excessive text.
* Unclear shop path.

---

# 20. Final Wireframe Decision

The homepage should feel like the entrance to the world of Gift Story.

It should not sell aggressively.

It should guide the customer gently from:

```text id="ih2abs"
Brand feeling → Current Story → Packaging trust → Shop or Special Request
```

The customer should leave the homepage understanding:

* Gift Story is a premium gifting brand.
* The current focus is Little Stories.
* Packaging is part of the value.
* The shop is handled through an official sales platform.
* Special requests have their own organized path.
