\# Little Stories Wireframe



\## Page Purpose



The Little Stories page presents Gift Story’s baby and kids gifting direction.



This page is not a full product catalog.

It should introduce the category, explain the gifting experience, highlight the current active story, and guide the visitor to shop through the official commerce platform.



\---



\## Page Role



Little Stories is the first active gifting category for Gift Story.



It focuses on refined baby and kids gifts, curated into limited stories with premium packaging and a ready-to-gift presentation.



\---



\## Primary User Goal



The visitor should understand:



1\. What Little Stories means.

2\. Why the gift feels special.

3\. What the current story is.

4\. How to shop or request the current story.

5\. That every story is limited and carefully curated.



\---



\## Layout Structure



\### 1. Hero Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Introduce Little Stories with a soft, emotional, premium feeling.



\*\*Content elements:\*\*



\* Small label: `Little Stories`

\* Main headline: `Tiny gifts, beautifully told.`

\* Supporting Arabic headline: `هدايا الصغار بتفاصيل تُحفظ في الذاكرة`

\* Short description:

&#x20; `Curated baby and kids gifts, wrapped inside the quiet world of Gift Story. Each story is prepared with care, softness, and a ready-to-gift presentation.`

\* Primary CTA:

&#x20; `Shop The Current Story`

\* Secondary CTA:

&#x20; `Discover The Packaging`



\*\*Visual direction:\*\*



\* Off-white or creamy background.

\* Soft gold stars or moon pattern.

\* One calm baby gift visual or packaging mockup.

\* No crowded product grid in the hero.



\---



\### 2. Category Promise Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Explain what makes Little Stories different from regular baby gifts.



\*\*Cards:\*\*



1\. `Curated With Care`



&#x20;  \* Products are selected to feel soft, useful, and gift-worthy.



2\. `Ready To Gift`



&#x20;  \* Every story is prepared inside Gift Story’s premium packaging.



3\. `Limited Stories`



&#x20;  \* Each collection is released in limited quantities and may not be repeated.



4\. `Personal Touch`



&#x20;  \* A gift card message can be added to complete the story.



\---



\### 3. Current Story Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Show the active story without turning the page into a crowded store.



\*\*Content elements:\*\*



\* Label: `Current Story`

\* Title: `Baby Story Box v1`

\* Arabic title: `صندوق قصة المولود الفاخر`

\* Status badge: `Limited Drop`

\* Short description:

&#x20; `A refined newborn gift story prepared inside Gift Story’s signature box, created for hospital visits, home welcomes, and meaningful congratulations.`

\* Price note:

&#x20; `Starting price and availability are confirmed through the active shop listing.`

\* CTA:

&#x20; `Shop The Current Story`



\*\*Important rule:\*\*



Do not show many product variations on this page.

Detailed products, prices, quantities, and checkout should stay inside the commerce platform.



\---



\### 4. What May Be Included Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Give the visitor a general idea without promising fixed contents.



\*\*Suggested list:\*\*



\* Selected baby clothing or accessories.

\* Gift Story signature box.

\* Inner wrapping paper.

\* Round sticker seal.

\* Gift card message.

\* Protective delivery packaging.



\*\*Important note:\*\*



`Contents may vary by story and availability while maintaining the same Gift Story standard.`



\---



\### 5. Packaging Experience Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Connect Little Stories to the Gift Story unboxing experience.



\*\*Content elements:\*\*



\* Title: `The Little Story Unboxing`

\* Description:

&#x20; `From the outer box to the inner card, every detail is designed to make the gift feel complete before it is even opened.`

\* Visual elements:



&#x20; \* Box front logo.

&#x20; \* Inner castle and slogan.

&#x20; \* Stars and moon tissue paper.

&#x20; \* Gift card.

&#x20; \* Round sticker.



\*\*Link:\*\*



CTA to packaging page or section:

`View Packaging Experience`



\---



\### 6. How It Works Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Explain the simple buying journey.



\*\*Steps:\*\*



1\. `Choose the current story`



&#x20;  \* Browse the active Little Stories drop.



2\. `Confirm details`



&#x20;  \* Select availability, gift message, and delivery details.



3\. `Gift Story prepares it`



&#x20;  \* The box is arranged, wrapped, and checked.



4\. `Ready for gifting`



&#x20;  \* The gift is delivered in protective packaging.



\---



\### 7. Trust \& Policy Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Set expectations clearly.



\*\*Content points:\*\*



\* Limited availability.

\* Contents may vary by story.

\* Gift messages must be confirmed before preparation.

\* No return or exchange after the order leaves the preparation center, except before preparation or in case of a clear fulfillment error.

\* Delivery packaging protects the box and is not part of the luxury unboxing.



\---



\### 8. Coming Little Stories Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Create anticipation without opening too many active categories.



\*\*Suggested cards:\*\*



1\. `Newborn Welcome`

2\. `Soft Baby Essentials`

3\. `Mini Celebration`

4\. `Seasonal Little Story`



\*\*Status:\*\*



Use `Coming Soon` labels only.

Do not add prices or product details before costing and approval.



\---



\### 9. Final CTA Section



\*\*Goal:\*\* Move the visitor to the shop.



\*\*Headline:\*\*



`A small gift can still tell a beautiful story.`



\*\*CTA:\*\*



`Shop The Current Story`



\*\*Secondary text:\*\*



`Availability is limited and may change by drop.`



\---



\## UX Notes



\* Keep the page calm and spacious.

\* Avoid heavy product grids.

\* Avoid discount language.

\* Avoid showing raw product photos with tags or source labels.

\* Use soft photography and clean product presentation.

\* Keep pricing and inventory inside the commerce platform.

\* Keep the official website focused on brand trust and storytelling.



\---



\## Visual Notes



Use the Gift Story visual system:



\* Off-White

\* Creamy Beige

\* Nude

\* Muted / Satin Gold

\* Soft gold stars and moon pattern

\* Castle motif for storytelling

\* Clean product photography

\* Minimal typography



Do not use navy as a primary identity color.

Do not use loud baby colors or cartoon-style graphics.
