# Gift Story

**Gift Story** is a premium curated gift-box brand based in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.

**Slogan:** WHERE EVERY GIFT TELLS A STORY

Gift Story is not positioned as a traditional product store. It is a curated lifestyle gifting experience built around limited drops, refined packaging, emotional storytelling, and ready-to-present luxury gift boxes.

---

## Project Status

This repository contains the launch-stage website and project documentation for Gift Story.

Current launch focus:

- Baby Story Box v1
- Premium newborn gift boxes
- Limited Drops model
- WhatsApp-based checkout
- Cloudflare Pages hosting
- Static HTML launch page before full platform expansion

## Core Product

### Baby Story Box v1

**Arabic name:** صندوق قصة المولود الفاخر  
**Launch price:** 299 SAR  
**Box size:** 40 × 30 × 10 cm  
**Main selling point:** A luxury gift box engineered as a complete handbag-style presentation box with satin ribbon handles.

The product is designed to be ready for gifting without requiring additional paper bags or external wrapping.

## Brand Direction

Gift Story follows a soft, elegant, and minimal luxury visual direction.

### Visual Identity

- Off-White
- Creamy Beige
- Nude
- Muted / Satin Gold
- Soft Pink for digital-only accents

### Print Rule

All physical print assets should follow a strict single-color production rule:

**Muted / Satin Gold Hot Foil Stamping**

No gradients, shadows, secondary colors, or low-resolution raster elements should be used in final print files.

## Return and Exchange Policy

Due to the nature of newborn products and customized gifts, Gift Story applies a strict no-return and no-exchange policy after an order leaves the preparation center.

Exceptions:

1. Cancellation before preparation or shipping.
2. A clear fulfillment error from Gift Story.
