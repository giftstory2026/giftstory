# Gift Story — Packaging System

## 1. Purpose of This File

This file defines the packaging system for Gift Story.

Its purpose is to make sure every gift presentation, print decision, supplier instruction, delivery protection, and customer-facing expectation stays consistent.

Packaging is not treated as a secondary detail in Gift Story.
Packaging is part of the gift value and part of the customer experience.

---

## 2. Packaging Philosophy

Gift Story packaging should feel:

* Elegant.
* Soft.
* Premium.
* Minimal.
* Gift-ready.
* Clean.
* Memorable.
* Connected to the story concept.

The packaging should make the customer feel that the gift is complete before they even see the products inside.

Gift Story packaging should never feel:

* Random.
* Cheap.
* Loud.
* Overdecorated.
* Childish.
* Crowded.
* Similar to generic gift packaging.

---

## 3. Packaging Structure

The Gift Story presentation includes:

1. **Branded outer gift bag / outer presentation bag**
2. **Branded inner box**
3. **Inner tissue paper**
4. **Sticker**
5. **Gift card**
6. **Products arranged inside**
7. **Protective nylon bag or shipping carton when needed**

Important clarification:

The protective nylon bag or shipping carton is not part of the luxury unboxing experience.
It is only used to protect the branded packaging during delivery or shipping.

---

## 4. Main Packaging Sizes

### Inner Box

Approved size:

**40 × 30 × 10 cm**

This is the main box used to hold the products and create the unboxing experience.

---

### Outer Gift Bag / Outer Presentation Bag

Approved size:

**45 × 33 × 15 cm**

This outer branded bag is used as part of the Gift Story presentation.

It should carry the Gift Story identity and protect the inner box visually as part of the gift handover.

---

### Protective Nylon Bag

The protective nylon bag is used only for logistics.

Its purpose is to protect the branded outer bag and inner box from:

* Rain.
* Dust.
* Delivery friction.
* Handling damage.
* Light dirt during transportation.
* Delivery app or courier handling.

It should not be treated as a luxury packaging element.

---

## 5. Inner Box Role

The inner box is the main gift container.

It should:

* Hold the products safely.
* Create the unboxing moment.
* Carry the Gift Story identity.
* Look clean and premium.
* Fit the selected products without crowding.
* Support the story concept visually.

The box should not look like a shipping box or storage carton.

---

## 6. Outer Gift Bag Role

The outer gift bag / outer presentation bag is part of the customer-facing presentation.

It should:

* Carry Gift Story branding.
* Look consistent with the inner box.
* Be clean and gift-appropriate.
* Protect the box visually.
* Make the handover look complete.

The outer gift bag should not be treated as a normal retail shopping bag.
It is part of the Gift Story presentation system.

---

## 7. Protective Packaging Role

Protective packaging includes:

* Nylon protection bag.
* Shipping carton.
* Internal shipping protection if needed.

This layer is used for delivery and logistics only.

It should protect:

* The branded outer bag.
* The inner box.
* Corners and edges.
* Satin details if used.
* Cleanliness of the gift presentation.

Customer-facing rule:

The customer should remove the protective layer and receive a clean Gift Story presentation.

---

## 8. Packaging Components

The full packaging system may include:

* Branded outer gift bag.
* Inner branded box.
* Tissue paper.
* Sticker.
* Gift card.
* Internal product support.
* Ribbon or satin detail if approved.
* Protective nylon bag.
* Shipping carton for out-of-city delivery.

Each component must have a clear role.
No packaging element should be added only for decoration if it creates clutter or increases cost without improving the experience.

---

## 9. Print Style

Physical packaging print should follow one production direction:

**Single-color muted / satin gold print**

This applies to:

* Outer gift bag.
* Inner box.
* Sticker.
* Gift card.
* Tissue paper pattern when printed.
* Any printed packaging insert.

The goal is to keep production premium, consistent, and controlled.

---

## 10. Digital vs Physical Color Rule

Gift Story digital visuals may include soft pink details, such as the heart accent in the logo.

However, physical printing does not need to match digital pink details.

Physical print should use:

**One unified muted / satin gold color**

This is not a defect.
It is part of the approved production standard.

---

## 11. Customer Terms Note

A clear note should be included in policies or product terms:

**Arabic customer-facing note:**

قد تختلف بعض التفاصيل اللونية بين العروض الرقمية والتغليف المطبوع، حيث تعتمد Gift Story في التغليف الفعلي على طباعة فاخرة بلون ذهبي موحد حسب مواصفات الإنتاج.

**English internal meaning:**

Some color details may differ between digital previews and printed packaging. Gift Story uses unified muted gold production printing for physical packaging according to approved production standards.

---

## 12. Logo Placement

### Outer Gift Bag

Gift Story logo should appear clearly on the outer presentation bag.

Recommended direction:

* Centered.
* Minimal.
* Printed in muted / satin gold.
* No crowded decoration around it.
* Same print direction on both main sides if approved.

---

### Inner Box Exterior

Gift Story logo should appear on the front or top-facing side of the box depending on final box structure.

Recommended direction:

* Centered.
* Calm.
* Muted / satin gold.
* No heavy pattern around it.
* Enough empty space around the logo.

---

## 13. Inner Box Interior

The inside of the box should create the story moment.

Recommended elements:

* Castle illustration.
* Slogan: WHERE EVERY GIFT TELLS A STORY
* Soft stars or moon elements if suitable.
* Muted / satin gold production color.

The interior should feel like the customer is opening a Gift Story world, not only a product container.

---

## 14. Tissue Paper

Tissue paper should support the unboxing experience.

Recommended direction:

* Soft creamy or off-white base.
* Stars and moon pattern.
* Light spacing.
* Not too crowded.
* Muted gold or soft gold effect.
* Folded neatly around the products.

The pattern should not overpower the products or the gift card.

---

## 15. Sticker

The sticker is used to close the tissue paper and complete the unboxing moment.

Recommended direction:

* Round sticker.
* Gift Story logo or castle.
* Muted gold print.
* Soft cream / off-white background.
* Clean margins.
* Premium finishing.

Preferred concept:

Use the castle for the sticker when the logo already appears on the outer packaging.

Reason:

The castle strengthens the story world and avoids repeating the logo too much.

---

## 16. Gift Card

The gift card is one of the most important emotional elements.

Recommended size:

**10 × 15 cm**

Recommended direction:

* Creamy or off-white base.
* Minimal front design.
* Gift Story logo or castle.
* Enough empty space.
* Back side suitable for writing.
* Muted / satin gold print.

The gift card should not be visually crowded.
It should feel like a premium personal message card.

---

## 17. Product Arrangement Inside the Box

Products should be arranged in a way that feels intentional.

Rules:

* Products should not be thrown inside the box.
* Larger items should support the layout.
* Smaller items should be placed with balance.
* Colors should feel coordinated.
* Tags or retail labels should not disturb the gift presentation.
* The arrangement should photograph well.
* The box should not look empty or overcrowded.

The customer should feel that the gift was curated, not assembled randomly.

---

## 18. Packaging Fit Rule

Before adding any product to a box, check:

* Does it fit the box size?
* Does it fit the story?
* Does it look premium inside the box?
* Does it affect the unboxing arrangement?
* Does it need internal support?
* Does it create shipping risk?
* Does it match the category?
* Does it justify the final price?

A product that does not present well should not be forced into the box.

---

## 19. Delivery Inside Jeddah

For local delivery inside Jeddah:

* Prepare the inner box.
* Place it inside the branded outer gift bag / presentation bag.
* Use protective nylon if needed.
* Make sure the branded packaging stays clean.
* Do not allow courier stickers to be placed directly on the branded gift presentation.
* Inform the courier that the package is a gift and should not be compressed.

The goal is for the customer or receiver to get a clean branded gift presentation.

---

## 20. Shipping Outside Jeddah

For shipping outside Jeddah:

* Prepare the full branded presentation.
* Protect the gift bag and inner box.
* Place the presentation inside a shipping carton when needed.
* Add internal support to prevent movement.
* Place shipping label only on the external shipping carton.
* Do not place shipping labels directly on the Gift Story branded packaging.

The goal is for the receiver to open the shipping carton and still find a clean premium Gift Story presentation.

---

## 21. Packaging Quality Checklist

Before any order leaves preparation, check:

* Outer gift bag is clean.
* Inner box is clean.
* Logo is visible and not damaged.
* Gold print is acceptable.
* Tissue paper is not torn.
* Sticker is centered and clean.
* Gift card is included.
* Gift card message is correct.
* Products are arranged neatly.
* No visible dust, glue marks, or damage.
* Protective nylon or shipping carton is used when needed.
* Customer delivery details are correct.
* The full presentation looks gift-ready.

---

## 22. Print Production Requirements

For print production, files should be:

* Vector-based when possible.
* Clean and high-resolution.
* Prepared for one-color printing.
* Free from unnecessary gradients.
* Free from shadows.
* Free from low-quality raster elements.
* Converted to outlines when required.
* Clearly named.
* Exported with print-ready PDF when needed.
* Supported by preview images for review.

---

## 23. Print Restrictions

Avoid in physical print:

* Multiple colors unless explicitly approved.
* Pink heart print as a separate required color.
* Gradients.
* Shadows.
* Glossy digital effects.
* Very thin lines.
* Very small unreadable details.
* Crowded patterns.
* Low-resolution files.
* Unclear logo versions.
* Overdecorated packaging.

## 25. Packaging Cost Rule

The current basic packaging cost assumption is approximately:

**18 SAR per full gift presentation**

This number should be treated as an estimate based on current supplier assumptions.

Final cost must be recalculated per batch depending on:

* Quantity.
* Supplier.
* Print cost.
* Outer bag cost.
* Inner box cost.
* Tissue paper cost.
* Sticker cost.
* Gift card cost.
* Protective material cost.
* Waste and rejected samples.
* Reprint needs.

Packaging cost must be included in the total cost before pricing.

---

## 26. Packaging and Pricing

Packaging is not a free extra.

It is part of the total cost and part of the perceived value of Gift Story.

Each gift price should account for:

* Product landed cost.
* Packaging cost.
* Platform/payment fees.
* Delivery channel fees if applicable.
* Preparation complexity.
* Target profit.

Gift Story should never price a box based only on product cost.

---

## 27. Packaging Boundaries

Do not approve packaging that:

* Looks generic.
* Feels cheap.
* Uses unrelated colors.
* Uses loud patterns.
* Looks childish in a non-premium way.
* Has poor print quality.
* Damages easily.
* Does not protect the products.
* Confuses the customer.
* Increases cost without improving value.

---

## 28. Final Packaging Decision

Gift Story’s approved packaging system is:

* Branded outer gift bag / presentation bag: **45 × 33 × 15 cm**
* Branded inner box: **40 × 30 × 10 cm**
* Soft tissue paper.
* Round sticker.
* Gift card.
* Single-color muted / satin gold print.
* Protective nylon or shipping carton only for logistics.

The customer-facing presentation must always look clean, premium, and complete.

The logistics protection must preserve the gift experience without becoming part of the luxury identity.
