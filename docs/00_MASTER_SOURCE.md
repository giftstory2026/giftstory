## 2\. Brand Basics

**Brand Name:** Gift Story
**Arabic Name:** قفت ستوري
**Slogan:** WHERE EVERY GIFT TELLS A STORY
**Location:** Jeddah, Saudi Arabia
**Brand Type:** Premium curated gifting brand
**Business Model:** Limited Drops + curated gift stories

Gift Story is not a normal product shop.
It is a premium gifting brand that turns selected products into complete gift stories through packaging, presentation, emotional detail, and limited availability.

\---

## 3\. Brand Essence

Gift Story sells the full gifting moment.

The brand value is built through:

* Curated products.
* Elegant packaging.
* A story around each gift.
* Ready-to-gift presentation.
* Personal message.
* Limited drops.

Gift Story does not sell things only.
Gift Story creates the moment around the gift.

## 12\. Pricing Model

Gift Story does not use one fixed price for all boxes.

The price depends on:

* Product landed cost.
* Packaging cost.
* Platform fees.
* Payment fees.
* Delivery app commission.
* Preparation complexity.
* Category value.
* Desired profit.
* Customer perception.

The 299 SAR price is not cancelled.
It remains a possible pricing tier, not a fixed rule for all boxes.

\---

## 13\. Profit Rule

Minimum target profit:

**50% above total cost**

Formula:

```text
Target Profit = Total Cost × 50%
```

```text
Minimum Net Revenue Needed = Total Cost × 1.5
```

This target is calculated above cost, not as 50% of the final selling price.

\---

## 14\. Cost Rule

Product cost should be calculated as:

**Landed Product Cost**

This means the real product cost after including any shipping, customs, travel, or sourcing costs if already embedded.

Do not count international shipping or customs twice.

\---

## 15\. Packaging Cost Rule

Current basic packaging cost assumption:

**Approximately 18 SAR per full gift presentation**

This is an estimate and must be recalculated per batch depending on:

* Supplier.
* Quantity.
* Print cost.
* Outer gift bag cost.
* Inner box cost.
* Tissue paper.
* Sticker.
* Gift card.
* Protective material.
* Waste and rejected samples.

Packaging is part of the total cost and must be included before pricing.

\---

## 16\. Payment Fee Assumption

Working assumption for platform payment:

```text
Payment fee = 7% + 1 SAR
```

This must be updated once the final platform and payment gateway terms are confirmed.

\---

## 17\. Delivery App Commission Assumption

Working assumption for delivery apps:

```text
Delivery app commission = 22.5%
```

This must be verified through the actual merchant contract for each platform.

If a platform deducts commission from the selling price, pricing must be calculated by division, not simple addition.

Example:

```text
If commission is 22.5%, remaining revenue is 77.5%.
Minimum App Price = Required Net Revenue ÷ 0.775
```

## 37\. Final Master Decision

Gift Story should be built as a premium brand system, not as a rushed online store.

The correct structure is:

* Official website for brand trust.
* Commerce platform for selling.
* Special request form for custom gifts.
* Limited drops for product focus.
* Clean packaging system.
* Variable pricing by cost and channel.
* 50% profit above cost as the minimum target.
* Baby/Kids as the first active category.
* Coming Soon cards for future categories.

Gift Story’s long-term value is not in having many products.

It is in making every gift feel like a story.
