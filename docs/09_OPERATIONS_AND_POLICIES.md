# Gift Story — Operations and Policies

## 1. Purpose of This File

This file defines the operational and customer policy system for Gift Story.

Its purpose is to make daily operations clear, organized, and aligned with the premium brand positioning.

Gift Story should operate with structure, not through random messages or informal order handling.

---

## 2. Operating Principle

Gift Story should feel premium not only in packaging, but also in the way it handles customers, orders, payments, delivery, and support.

The customer should feel that Gift Story is:

* Organized.
* Trustworthy.
* Clear.
* Professional.
* Premium.
* Careful with details.
* Consistent in communication.

Operations should protect the brand image as much as the packaging does.

---

## 3. Sales Operation Model

Gift Story uses a two-layer operating model:

### Layer 1 — Official Website

The official website is used for:

* Brand presentation.
* Gift categories.
* Packaging experience.
* Previous stories.
* Special Gift Story Request.
* Policies.
* Contact.
* Redirecting customers to the active sales platform.

### Layer 2 — Commerce Platform

The commerce platform is used for:

* Active products.
* Prices.
* Inventory.
* Checkout.
* Payment.
* Orders.
* Invoices.
* Delivery settings.
* Customer notifications.

Preferred starting platform:

**Salla**, unless Zid is later selected for better operational reasons.

---

## 4. Customer Ordering Flow — Standard Product

Recommended standard order flow:

1. Customer visits the official website.
2. Customer explores the brand and current story.
3. Customer clicks **Shop The Current Story**.
4. Customer is redirected to the active commerce platform.
5. Customer selects the available product.
6. Customer completes checkout through the platform.
7. Gift Story receives the order details.
8. Gift Story prepares the gift.
9. Gift Story protects the branded packaging for delivery.
10. Order is delivered or shipped.
11. Customer receives the gift presentation.

The standard purchase should not depend on public WhatsApp ordering.

---

## 5. Customer Ordering Flow — Special Request

Special requests should be handled through a dedicated form.

Page name:

**Special Gift Story Request**

Arabic name:

**طلب قصة خاصة**

Recommended flow:

1. Customer opens the Special Gift Story Request page.
2. Customer submits the form.
3. Gift Story reviews the request.
4. Gift Story replies by email.
5. If needed, WhatsApp may be shared selectively.
6. Gift concept and estimated price are confirmed.
7. Payment is completed through an official method or payment link.
8. Gift Story prepares the request.
9. Order is delivered or shipped.

Important:

Special requests are not instant orders.
They require review, availability check, pricing, and preparation time.

---

## 6. Special Request Policy

Special requests are subject to:

* Product availability.
* Preparation capacity.
* Delivery city.
* Time needed.
* Budget suitability.
* Brand fit.
* Packaging fit.
* Feasibility of the idea.

Customer-facing note:

**Arabic:**

الطلبات الخاصة تحتاج وقتًا إضافيًا للتجهيز، وتعتمد على توفر القطع المناسبة وإمكانية تنفيذ الفكرة.

**English internal meaning:**

Special requests require additional preparation time and depend on product availability and execution feasibility.

---

## 7. Special Request Form Status

The form can be:

### 1. Active

Used when Gift Story can accept custom requests.

### 2. Campaign Active

Used before travel, seasonal sourcing, or special limited request windows.

### 3. Paused

Used when capacity is full or custom requests are temporarily unavailable.

If paused, do not remove the page completely.
Show a premium paused message.

Suggested Arabic message:

طلبات القصص الخاصة غير متاحة حاليًا. نعمل على تجهيز قصص جديدة، ويمكنك متابعة الإصدارات القادمة قريبًا.

## 10. Payment Policy

Customer payments should preferably happen through official checkout methods.

Preferred payment methods:

* Commerce platform checkout.
* Mada.
* Visa / Mastercard.
* Apple Pay if available.
* Tabby / Tamara if approved and suitable.

Reason:

Official checkout improves trust, customer confidence, and brand professionalism.

Possible internal use cases:

* Platform settlements.
* Business payments.
* Supplier payments.

---

## 13. Gift Card Message Policy

Gift card messages must be handled carefully.

Rules:

* Do not write or print the message before confirmation.
* Preserve the customer’s wording unless correction is approved.
* Review names and dates.
* If the message is too long, inform the customer politely.
* Keep the card clean and premium.
* Do not publish personal messages without permission.

For special or sensitive orders, confirm the final message with the customer before preparation.

---

## 14. Product Preparation Standards

Before placing products inside a Gift Story box, check:

* Product is clean.
* Product is unused and suitable.
* No visible damage.
* No unpleasant smell.
* Size and category are correct.
* Product matches the story.
* Product fits the box.
* Product looks premium when arranged.
* Retail tags or price labels do not disturb the presentation.
* Product is safe and appropriate for its category.

Products should never be placed randomly inside the box.

---

## 15. Packaging Preparation Flow

Recommended packaging flow:

1. Confirm order details.
2. Confirm selected products.
3. Confirm gift card message.
4. Prepare inner box.
5. Arrange products neatly.
6. Add tissue paper.
7. Close tissue with sticker.
8. Add gift card.
9. Final visual check.
10. Place inner box inside branded outer gift bag.
11. Add protective nylon or shipping carton if needed.
12. Attach shipping label only to external protective packaging.
13. Send order to delivery or shipping.

---

## 16. Packaging Protection Rule

Gift Story branded packaging must be protected during delivery and shipping.

The following are part of the gift presentation:

* Branded outer gift bag.
* Branded inner box.
* Tissue paper.
* Sticker.
* Gift card.

The following are logistics protection only:

* Nylon protection bag.
* Shipping carton.
* Internal shipping support.

Shipping labels should not be placed directly on branded packaging.

---

## 17. Delivery Inside Jeddah

For local delivery inside Jeddah:

* Prepare the full branded gift presentation.
* Use protective nylon if needed.
* Protect the outer gift bag and inner box.
* Do not allow courier labels on the branded gift packaging.
* Make sure the order is not compressed.
* Inform the courier that the item is a gift.
* Deliver in a clean and presentable condition.

The customer should receive the branded presentation clean and intact.

---

## 18. Shipping Outside Jeddah

For shipping outside Jeddah:

* Prepare the full branded gift presentation.
* Place it inside a shipping carton when needed.
* Add internal protection to reduce movement.
* Place shipping label on the external carton only.
* Make sure the branded packaging remains clean.
* Avoid pressure that may damage the box or bag.

The receiver should open the shipping carton and find a clean Gift Story presentation inside.

---

## 19. Delivery App Orders

For orders through delivery apps such as HungerStation, ToYou, or The Chefz:

* Keep the product selection limited and easy to prepare.
* Use channel-specific pricing.
* Protect the branded presentation carefully.
* Make preparation time realistic.
* Avoid offering products that need long customization.
* Do not list items that cannot be fulfilled quickly.
* Ensure app images are clean and customer-facing.

Delivery app orders should be operationally simple.

---

## 20. Product Availability Policy

Gift Story uses limited drops and curated availability.

Customer-facing rule:

* Products may be limited.
* Some stories may not be repeated.
* Availability depends on stock.
* Previous stories may be used as inspiration but may not be available again exactly.

Suggested Arabic note:

بعض القصص تكون محدودة وقد لا تتكرر بنفس التفاصيل، لكن يمكن استخدامها كمرجع للإلهام أو الطلبات الخاصة حسب توفر القطع.

---

## 21. Return and Exchange Policy

Due to the nature of gift items, baby/kids products, prepared packaging, and personalized presentation, return and exchange may be restricted after the order leaves preparation.

Customer-facing Arabic policy:

نظرًا لطبيعة الهدايا المجهزة ومنتجات الأطفال والتغليف المخصص، لا يمكن استرجاع أو استبدال الطلب بعد خروجه من مقر التجهيز، حفاظًا على جودة المنتجات وسلامة تجربة الإهداء.

Exceptions may include:

1. Cancellation before preparation or dispatch.
2. Clear fulfillment error from Gift Story.
3. Any case required by applicable regulation.

All exception cases should be reviewed and documented.

---

## 22. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation may be possible only if:

* The order has not entered preparation.
* The gift card message has not been prepared.
* Products have not been assembled.
* The order has not been dispatched.
* No special sourcing has started for a custom request.

Special requests may have different cancellation rules depending on preparation progress.

---

## 23. Print and Color Variation Policy

Gift Story physical packaging uses one-color muted / satin gold printing.

Digital visuals may include soft pink details or digital effects.

Customer-facing Arabic note:

قد تختلف بعض التفاصيل اللونية بين العروض الرقمية والتغليف المطبوع، حيث تعتمد Gift Story في التغليف الفعلي على طباعة فاخرة بلون ذهبي موحد حسب مواصفات الإنتاج.

This protects the brand from misunderstandings when digital previews differ from physical print.

---

## 24. Product Variation Policy

Because Gift Story uses limited drops and curated products, some contents may vary depending on availability.

Customer-facing Arabic note:

قد تختلف بعض تفاصيل المحتوى حسب توفر القطع، مع الحفاظ على نفس مستوى التنسيق والفخامة في كل قصة.

This note should be used carefully and only when relevant.

---

## 25. Photography and Documentation

Gift Story should photograph selected orders before delivery when possible.

Reasons:

* Document order condition.
* Support customer service.
* Create content.
* Track packaging quality.
* Protect against unclear complaints.

Do not publish:

* Customer personal information.
* Gift card message.
* Recipient name.
* Address.
* Phone number.

Do not publish customer content without permission.

---

## 26. Quality Checklist Before Dispatch

Before any order leaves preparation, check:

* Correct order.
* Correct product.
* Correct quantity.
* Correct gift card message.
* Products are clean.
* Products are arranged well.
* Tissue paper is clean and not torn.
* Sticker is centered and clean.
* Gift card is included.
* Inner box is clean.
* Outer gift bag is clean.
* Print is acceptable.
* Protective packaging is used if needed.
* Delivery information is correct.
* No price tags or purchase source labels are visible.
* No shipping label is attached to branded gift packaging.
* Final presentation looks premium.

---

## 27. Customer Support Style

Customer support should sound:

* Formal.
* Clear.
* Warm.
* Helpful.
* Calm.
* Premium.
* Direct.

Avoid:

* Very casual replies.
* Slow replies without explanation.
* Overpromising.
* Unclear pricing.
* Blaming the customer.
* Using too many emojis.
* Publicly sharing WhatsApp as the first solution.
* Informal payment instructions as the main method.

---

## 28. Complaint Handling

Complaint handling steps:

1. Thank the customer for reaching out.
2. Ask for order details.
3. Ask for photos if needed.
4. Review order documentation.
5. Check preparation photos if available.
6. Identify whether it is a fulfillment error, delivery issue, or expectation issue.
7. Respond clearly.
8. Offer a suitable solution when applicable.
9. Document the case.

Suggested Arabic opening:

نعتذر لك عن أي تجربة لم تكن بالمستوى الذي نطمح له في Gift Story. نرجو تزويدنا بتفاصيل الطلب وصورة توضح المشكلة، وسنراجع الحالة بعناية ونعود لك بالرد المناسب.

---

## 36. Operational Red Flags

Review operations if:

* Orders depend too much on manual WhatsApp.
* Prices are unclear.
* Products are added without cost tracking.
* Packaging cost is ignored.
* Delivery protection is skipped.
* Customer messages are not confirmed.
* Gift card messages contain mistakes.
* Products do not fit the box.
* Photos are weak.
* App orders are hard to fulfill quickly.
* Policies are not visible.
* Custom requests are accepted without capacity.

---

## 37. Final Operations Decision

Gift Story should operate as a premium, organized, and platform-based gifting brand.

Core decisions:

* Website builds trust and brand value.
* Commerce platform handles orders and payment.
* Special requests use a dedicated form.
* WhatsApp is secondary support, not the main public storefront.
* Packaging must be protected during delivery.
* Prices are channel-specific.
* Policies must be clear.
* Every box must be costed before sale.
* Every order must leave preparation looking complete and premium.

The operational goal is simple:

**Every Gift Story order should feel intentional, protected, and worthy of the occasion.**
