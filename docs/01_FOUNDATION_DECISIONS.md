# Gift Story — Foundation Decisions

## 1. Purpose of This File

This file defines the core foundation decisions for Gift Story.

It is the first reference file for the new clean source structure.
All previous files are considered archive references only if they conflict with the decisions listed here.

The goal is to build Gift Story from a clear, organized, and scalable foundation.

---

## 2. Brand Definition

**Brand Name:** Gift Story
**Arabic Name:** قفت ستوري
**Slogan:** WHERE EVERY GIFT TELLS A STORY
**Location:** Jeddah, Saudi Arabia
**Brand Type:** Luxury curated gifting brand
**Business Model:** Limited Drops + curated gift experiences

Gift Story is not positioned as a normal product store.
It is a premium gifting experience built around stories, presentation, limited availability, and elegant packaging.

---

## 3. What Gift Story Actually Sells

Gift Story does not only sell products inside a box.

Gift Story sells:

* A curated gift experience.
* A luxury presentation.
* A ready-to-gift story.
* A memorable unboxing moment.
* Carefully selected items.
* Limited and changing gift drops.

The customer is buying the full emotional and visual experience, not only the physical products.

---

## 4. Launch Focus

The first visible launch category will focus on:

**Little Stories — Baby & Kids Gifts**
**Arabic customer-facing name:** هدايا الصغار

This category is selected first because it matches the currently available product direction and is easier to explain to customers during launch.

Other categories may appear on the official website as Coming Soon cards, but they should not be opened for sales until ready.

## 6. Current Drop Button

The main order button on the official website should be:

**English:** Shop The Current Story
**Arabic:** تسوّقي القصة الحالية

This wording is preferred because it matches the Gift Story brand concept better than a normal commercial phrase like “Shop Now”.

---

## 7. Special Requests

Special requests should have a dedicated page on the official website.

**Page Name:** Special Gift Story Request
**Arabic customer-facing name:** طلب قصة خاصة

Purpose:

* Custom gift boxes.
* Special occasions.
* Travel-related requests.
* Pre-travel custom orders.
* Personalized gift concepts.
* Requests that need extra preparation time.

The form can be active all the time or enabled during specific campaigns.

Important note for customers:

Special requests require additional preparation time and are subject to availability.

---

## 8. Website Role

The official website should not be treated as a simple one-page sales page.

The official website should act as the main brand home.

Its role:

* Present Gift Story as a luxury gifting brand.
* Show the story, visual identity, and packaging experience.
* Display previous and sample gift stories.
* Introduce categories.
* Show Coming Soon drops.
* Host the Special Gift Story Request page.
* Direct customers to the official sales platform for available products.

The official website should not display detailed product prices in the main brand pages.

---

## 9. Commerce Role

Actual selling, payment, inventory, and order management should be handled through a commerce platform such as Salla or Zid.

Preferred starting option:

**Salla**, unless later comparison shows Zid is operationally better.

The commerce platform should handle:

* Product listings.
* Active drop prices.
* Inventory.
* Orders.
* Payment gateways.
* Invoices.
* Customer checkout.
* Delivery settings.
* Discounts if needed.
* Platform-specific pricing.

---

## 10. Pricing Visibility

Prices should not be shown on the official showcase website by default.

Prices should appear inside each sales channel only, because each channel may have different pricing.

Examples:

* Salla / Zid price.
* HungerStation price.
* ToYou price.
* The Chefz price.
* Any future delivery platform price.

This prevents confusion and allows each channel to absorb its own fees and commissions.

---

## 11. Pricing Principle

Gift Story prices are not fixed at 299 SAR.

The price of each box depends on:

* Actual product cost.
* Packaging cost.
* Platform fees.
* Payment fees.
* Delivery channel commission.
* Desired profit.
* Gift complexity.
* Category and occasion.
* Customization level.

The 299 SAR price can remain as one possible tier, but it should not be treated as the fixed price for all boxes.

---

## 12. Profit Rule

The minimum target profit is:

**50% above total cost**

This means:

If the total cost of a gift box is 200 SAR, the minimum target profit is 100 SAR, before adjusting for platform and payment fees if needed.

Total cost may include:

* Landed product cost.
* Packaging cost.
* Printing cost.
* Stickers, cards, tissue, ribbons, and protection materials.
* Platform fees when applicable.
* Payment gateway fees.
* Delivery app commissions when applicable.
* Any special preparation cost.

---

## 13. Product Cost Rule

Product cost should be calculated as:

**Landed Product Cost**

This means that if international shipping, customs, or travel-related costs are already included in the product cost, they should not be counted again as separate items.

The product database should calculate the real cost of each item before adding it to any box.

---

## 14. Packaging Cost Rule

The current basic packaging assumption is around:

**18 SAR per box**

This is based on current supplier assumptions and includes core packaging elements.

However, the final packaging cost must be calculated per batch because costs may change depending on:

* Supplier.
* Quantity.
* Printing method.
* Bag and box quality.
* Sticker and card production.
* Packaging waste.
* Reprints or sample production.

---

## 15. Packaging Structure

The approved size of the main box is:

**40 × 30 × 10 cm**

The approved size of the branded outer gift bag is:

**45 × 33 × 15 cm**

Packaging clarification:

* The Gift Story outer bag and inner box are part of the gift presentation.
* The nylon bag or shipping carton is only for logistical protection.
* The protective nylon bag is not part of the luxury unboxing experience.
* The purpose of protective packaging is to protect the branded bag and box from rain, dust, delivery friction, and shipping damage.

---

## 16. Print Rule

Physical printing should follow the approved production rule:

**Single-color muted / satin gold printing**

This applies to:

* Box.
* Outer bag.
* Sticker.
* Gift card.
* Tissue pattern when printed.
* Packaging elements.

Digital assets may include soft pink details such as the heart accent, but printed packaging does not need to match every digital color detail.

A terms note should clarify that printed packaging may use one unified gold color according to production standards.

---

## 17. WhatsApp Role

WhatsApp should not be the main public sales channel.

The preferred customer journey is:

1. Customer visits official website.
2. Customer views brand and gift stories.
3. Customer clicks Shop The Current Story.
4. Customer completes the purchase through the commerce platform.
5. Customer support may share WhatsApp only when needed.

WhatsApp may be used for:

* Selected support cases.
* Follow-up after email.
* Complex special requests.
* Delivery coordination if needed.
* Manual clarification when required.

---

## 18. Payment Rule

Gift Story should appear professional and organized in front of customers.

Customer payment should preferably happen through:

* Commerce platform checkout.
* Official payment gateway.
* Mada / cards.
* Apple Pay if available.
* Tabby / Tamara if approved and suitable.
* Platform-supported payment options.

Direct bank transfer should not be the primary customer-facing method, even if it is cheaper, because it may reduce the premium and professional perception of the brand.

---

## 19. Coming Soon Categories

Coming Soon categories should be shown as elegant visual cards on the official website.

They should include:

* Category name.
* Short emotional description.
* Visual direction or sample image.
* Coming Soon label.

They should not include:

* Prices.
* Exact launch dates unless confirmed.
* Products that are not ready.
* Promises that cannot be fulfilled.

---

## 20. Non-Negotiable Decisions

The following decisions should not be changed without a clear reason:

* Gift Story is a premium gifting brand, not a discount product store.
* The brand is built around stories, not normal product listings.
* The first launch focus is Baby / Kids gifts.
* Prices are not fixed across all boxes.
* Profit target is 50% above cost.
* The official website is for brand presence and storytelling.
* Sales operations should happen through a commerce platform.
* Website prices should be avoided unless there is a clear reason.
* Packaging must remain elegant, minimal, and aligned with the brand.
* Physical print uses one muted/satin gold color.
* WhatsApp is not the main public sales channel.
* Special requests should be managed through a dedicated form.
